Initial Data Exploration Plan

1) Player Efficiency & Usage
- Compute a usage proxy: (FGA + 0.44 * FTA + TOV) / MIN
- Compute a scoring efficiency proxy: PTS / (2 * (FGA + 0.44 * FTA))
- Filter to players with MIN >= 10 in at least 15 games
- Scatter plot: usage vs. efficiency, labeled for standout players
- Goal: identify high-usage efficient scorers vs. volume scorers dragging down efficiency

2) Home/Away Performance Splits
- Parse MATCHUP column: "vs." = home, "@" = away
- For each player (min games filter), compute average PTS, AST, REB, FG_PCT at home vs. away
- Visualize with paired bar charts or violin plots
- Goal: find players with notable home/away performance gaps

3) Rest Day Impact on Shooting
- Join fact_player_games with dim_schedule on (GAME_ID, TEAM_ID)
- Group by DAYS_REST bucket: 0 (back-to-back), 1, 2, 3+
- Compare FG_PCT, FG3_PCT, and PTS across rest buckets
- Visualize with boxplots per rest bucket
- Goal: quantify how fatigue (back-to-backs) affects shooting performance
